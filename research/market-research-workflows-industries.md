## PART 1: TOP 25 WORKFLOWS AGENCIES ARE AUTOMATING

### TIER 1: HIGHEST DEMAND (80% of agency revenue)

#### 1. Client Onboarding Automation
**Industry:** Universal (all professional services)
**Time Saved:** 8-12 hours per client
**Tools:** Make, Zapier, HubSpot, custom GPTs

**Workflow Components:**
- Intake form → CRM entry
- Automatic welcome email sequence
- Document collection requests
- Task creation in project management
- Calendar scheduling
- Contract/proposal generation
- Team notification and assignment

**ROI Example:**
- Law firm: 50 new clients/year × 10 hours = 500 hours saved
- At $150/hour = $75,000 annual value
- Implementation cost: $8,000
- ROI: 938%

#### 3. Meeting Intelligence & Follow-Up

4. Draft follow-up email

6. Update CRM with meeting notes

## PART 3: USE CASE DEEP DIVES

### USE CASE 1: Law Firm Client Intake

**Current State:**
- Partner receives referral call
- Manual intake form (30 min)
- Data entry into practice management (15 min)
- Conflict check (20 min)
- Engagement letter drafting (45 min)
- Back-and-forth for signatures (3-5 emails)
- File setup (15 min)
- **Total: 2.5-3 hours per client**

**Automated Workflow:**
1. Prospect fills online intake form
2. AI analyzes for case type and urgency
3. Auto-conflict check against database
4. Engagement letter auto-generated
5. E-signature request sent
6. Calendar hold for consultation
7. File auto-created in system
8. Team notifications sent
9. **Total: 15 minutes of review**

**Time Saved:** 2+ hours per client
**At 50 clients/year:** 100+ hours
**Value:** $20,000+ (at $200/hr)
