# AI Agency — Business Name Ideas

## Selection Criteria

1. **Available .com domain** (or strong alternative)
2. **Easy to spell and pronounce**
3. **Memorable**
4. **Conveys AI + business improvement**
5. **Professional but not corporate-stuffy**
6. **Local Chicago connection (nice-to-have)**
