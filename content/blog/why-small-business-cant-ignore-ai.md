## The Cost of Waiting

Let's look at the math on just one automation: instant lead response.

**Scenario A (No automation):**
- Lead comes in at 8 PM
- You respond at 9 AM (13 hours later)
- Lead has already talked to 2 competitors
- Close rate: 15%

**Scenario B (With automation):**
- Lead comes in at 8 PM
- Instant auto-reply at 8:00 PM
- Personal follow-up at 9 AM
- Lead feels prioritized, hasn't talked to competitors yet
- Close rate: 35%

**On 20 leads per month at $2,000 average deal:**
- Without automation: 3 closes = $6,000
- With automation: 7 closes = $14,000

**That's $8,000 per month in lost revenue from ONE automation.**
