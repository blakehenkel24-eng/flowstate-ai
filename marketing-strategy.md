## 6. SALES PROCESS

### Consultation Call Structure (30 min)

**1. Discovery (10 min)**
- Current workflow challenges
- What they've tried
- Goals and timeline
- Budget range

**2. Education (10 min)**
- How AI could help their specific situation
- Similar client examples
- Implementation approach

**3. Recommendation (5 min)**
- Suggest starting point (audit vs implementation)
- Pricing
- Timeline

**4. Next Steps (5 min)**
- Send proposal within 24 hours
- Schedule follow-up
- Answer immediate questions

### Proposal Template

**Structure:**
1. Executive Summary (1 page)
2. Current State Analysis
3. Recommended AI Workflows
4. Implementation Plan
5. Investment & ROI
6. Timeline
7. Next Steps

**Pricing Presentation:**
- Show 3 options: Audit / Implementation / Retainer
- Middle option is the target
- Include ROI calculation

### Closing Tactics

**Objection Handling:**

*"We tried AI before and it didn't work"*
→ "Most DIY AI fails because of poor setup and integration. We handle the technical complexity so you get results. Plus, 30-day guarantee."

*"We don't have budget for this"*
→ "What's the cost of 10 hours/week of your team's time? At $50/hour, that's $2K/month. This pays for itself in month 1."

*"We can just use ChatGPT"*
→ "ChatGPT is a tool. We build systems—integrated workflows that actually save time, not create more work."

*"We need to think about it"*
→ "Of course. What's your biggest concern? Let's address it now."

## 10. COMPETITIVE BATTLECARDS

### Battlecard: Big 4 Consultancies (Deloitte, Accenture)

**Their Positioning:**
- Enterprise AI transformation
- $500K+ engagements
- 6-12 month timelines

**Their Strengths:**
- Brand recognition
- Enterprise relationships
- Full-service capabilities

**Their Weaknesses:**
- Too expensive for SMBs
- Slow implementation
- Generic solutions
- Junior-heavy teams

**Our Advantages:**
- 1/10th the cost
- Results in 30 days
- Local presence
- Founder-led implementation

**When We Win:**
- Client is 20-200 employees
- Needs results fast
- Wants personal attention
- Budget is $5K-$50K

**Talk Track:**
"The Big 4 are great for Fortune 500s with $1M+ budgets. For a 50-person firm, you need someone who can move fast, stay focused, and not bury you in bureaucracy. That's us."

---

### Battlecard: DIY AI Tools (ChatGPT, etc.)

**Their Positioning:**
- Low-cost AI access
- Self-serve

**Their Strengths:**
- Cheap ($20/mo)
- Immediate access
- Flexible

**Their Weaknesses:**
- No integration with existing tools
- Generic outputs
- Requires prompt engineering expertise
- No measurement/optimization

**Our Advantages:**
- Integrated workflows
- Customized to your business
- Measured ROI
- Done-for-you

**When We Win:**
- Client tried DIY and failed
- Needs integration with CRM/email/docs
- Values time over $20/mo savings

**Talk Track:**
"ChatGPT is a tool. We build systems. The question isn't whether you can access AI—it's whether AI is actually saving you time. Most DIY attempts create more work. We fix that."
